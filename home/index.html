Empower Your Studies with Notes-Library

Introducing Notes-Library, your dedicated companion web-app for accessing a curated collection of digital textbooks in Statistics, Nutrition, and the Life Sciences. Created by a community of educators passionate about making quality learning materials freely accessible, Notes-Library brings well-structured, easy-to-read textbooks directly into your Google Workspace environment. Enjoy a clean, distraction-free reading experience with elegant typography, organized page layouts, and mobile-friendly formatting that lets you study anytime, anywhere. Whether you're a student preparing for exams, an instructor referencing course material, or a lifelong learner exploring new subjects.

Key Features

Effortless Digital Distribution

Share your educational materials with students and colleagues instantly. Notes-Library eliminates the hassle of distributing bulky PDFs or printed copies, delivering textbook content directly through a clean, accessible digital interface.

Always Up-to-Date Content

Unlike static PDF files that quickly become outdated, our digitally served textbooks allow educators to update information on the fly. Readers always access the most current version of the material, ensuring accuracy and relevance with every visit.

Seamless Reader Insights

Because users sign in with their Google accounts, contributing educators can easily keep track of who is engaging with their materials. This makes it simple to gather feedback, understand readership, and stay connected with your audience.

Interactive Reference Links

Enrich your textbooks with clickable links to external resources, research papers, supplementary readings, and multimedia content. Notes-Library turns traditional reference lists into interactive learning pathways that deepen understanding.

Clean, Focused Reading Experience

Enjoy elegantly formatted pages designed for readability across devices, allowing students to focus on learning without the clutter of unnecessary distractions.

Why Choose Notes-Library

Notes-Library is a small collaborative effort by a group of educators in Statistics, Nutrition and the Life Sciences who believe in making quality learning materials easily accessible to their students and peers. Rather than a large public platform, it's a focused space where a handful of instructors share their own textbooks digitally in a more convenient, modern format. If you're an educator with course material you'd like to share through our add-on, we'd love to hear from you — feel free to reach out and we can explore adding your content to our growing collection.
